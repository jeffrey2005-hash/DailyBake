About Us

Welcome to Daily Bake! We are a group of seven students who developed this website to showcase our creativity, teamwork, and web development skills. Our goal is to create a simple and enjoyable online experience where customers can explore a variety of delicious bakery products.

Daily Bake was designed with passion and dedication. Through this project, we applied our knowledge in HTML, CSS, and modern web design to create a visually appealing and user-friendly platform.

As students, we believe that collaboration and continuous learning are the keys to success. This project allowed us to strengthen our skills, solve problems together, and gain valuable experience in developing a real-world website.

Thank you for visiting Daily Bake. We hope you enjoy exploring our bakery collection as much as we enjoyed creating this project.
